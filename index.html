Mystery Box Guessing Game

Rules & Logic

  • There are 9 mystery boxes.
  • You have 5 trials to select boxes.
  • Each box may contain a prize, a trap, or be empty.
  • Finding a prize gives you 10 points.
  • Finding a trap adds to your losses.
  • Empty boxes give no points or losses.
  • Try to score 30 or more points to win the game.
  • The game ends after 5 trials, and the winner is announced.
  • You can click "Play Again" anytime to restart the game.
